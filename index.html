Our chosen solutions

The solutions that we chose are Soil Compost Amendment, Permeable Pavement and Storm Drain Management.

Permeable pavement can help absorb small pollutants from entering the storm drains.

Storm Drain Management

How does it work?

Soil Compost Amendment

Soil Compost Amendment works because compost absorbs more water than packed soil.

How it can fix erosion

Soil compost Amendment uses compost to replace the soil. By doing this, it can improve soil structure, and absorbs more water. By doing this, it also reduces erosion.

Other uses

Since Soil Copost Amendment uses compost, it means that it can provide nutrients to plants. Another way it can benifits is that it reduces runoff and can even act as a buffer zone.

Permeable Pavement

Permeable pavement works by letting water flow through the permeable joints, which flows through all the permeable material until it reaches the reservoir.

How it fixes pollutants in runoff

There are bacteria living in each layer that will assist in breaking down pollutants. Big objects will not be able to go to the reservoir as it does not allow big objects to flow through.

Other uses

Permeable pavements can not only be used to slow down runoff, but can also be used to pick up pollutants from the runoff.
